## FAQ

**How long do referral cookies last?**  
30 days. If someone clicks your link and buys within 30 days, you get credit.

**When do I get paid?**  
Monthly, around the 15th. Minimum payout is $50.

**Can I see who I referred?**  
You'll see aggregate stats (clicks, conversions, earnings) but not individual customer details.

**What if someone requests a refund?**  
Refunded sales are deducted from your commission balance.

**Is there a limit to how much I can earn?**  
No cap. Some affiliates earn $500+/month.
