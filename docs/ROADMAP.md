## Feature Prioritization Framework

We use the **RICE** framework to prioritize features:

**RICE = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort**

### Current Top Priorities (Q1 2025)

| Feature | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | RICE Score |
|---------|-------|--------|------------|--------|------------|
| 50-state support | 100% | 3 | 90% | 8 | 33.75 |
| BOI automation | 100% | 3 | 95% | 5 | 57.00 |
| Tax engine | 90% | 3 | 85% | 6 | 38.25 |
| AI Coach | 70% | 3 | 70% | 10 | 14.70 |
| Insurance | 60% | 2 | 80% | 7 | 13.71 |
| Community | 80% | 2 | 75% | 8 | 15.00 |

**Legend:**
- Reach: % of users who will use this
- Impact: 1 (low) to 3 (high)
- Confidence: % sure about estimates
- Effort: Person-weeks
- RICE: Priority score (higher = do first)

## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Responses

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Regulatory changes | High | Medium | Legal team monitoring + flexible architecture |
| Competition | High | High | Speed + quality + community moat |
| Technical debt | Medium | Medium | 20% time on refactoring |
| Scaling issues | High | Low | Load testing + over-provisioning |
| Key person dependency | Medium | Low | Documentation + cross-training |
| Partner issues | Medium | Medium | Multiple partners per category |
